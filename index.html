Self-immolation of Thích Quảng Đức (Saigon, Vietnam)

Quảng Đức burned himself to death at a busy Saigon road intersection on 6/11/1963, Quảng Đức was protesting the persecution of Buddhists by the South Vietnamese government.

Rubber Production for 1904 (Colonial Congo)

Nsala stares at his daughter's dismembered limbs. She was killed and chopped as a punishment when his villaged failed to meet the rubber quotas demanded by the Colonial Regime.

Eruption of Mount Pinatubo (Zambales, Philipines)

A pickup truck flees from the pyroclastic flows spewing from the volcano when it erupted on 6/11/1991. They survived.
